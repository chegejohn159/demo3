About Us

Dual Purpose Chicken project is a community based project that was conceptualized by four passionate, committed and enthusiastic entrepreneurs.

This exciting project is the brain child of Chick- Dee company limited. Dual Purpose Chicken Project was set up to promote job creation, women and youth empowerment, household economic development and poverty alleviation.

It features key stakeholders along the chicken value chain specifically women, youth and the wider community.

The project will specialize in the processing and sale of day old and one-month old chicks process and marketing of mature birds. The project will essentially hatch and brood slow growing broilers (kienyeji) and layers through their productive cycle.
